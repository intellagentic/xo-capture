Below is a comprehensive UK Fire Engineering Standards Library to serve as the backend reference database for the
software. I have structured this to mirror the internal knowledge bases used by Tier-1 consultancies, ensuring it can be
effectively indexed, queried, and linked to calculations or report outputs.
This list covers approximately 120 key UK standards, regulations, and technical guidance documents:
1. Statutory Legislation & Regulatory Framework
These should be treated as primary authority documents:
  - Building Act 1984
  - Building Regulations 2010 (as amended)
  - Building Safety Act 2022
  - Higher-Risk Buildings (Key Building Information etc.) Regulations 2023
  - Building (Higher-Risk Buildings Procedures) Regulations 2023
  - Regulatory Reform (Fire Safety) Order 2005
  - Fire Safety Act 2021
  - Fire Safety (England) Regulations 2022
  - Health and Safety at Work Act 1974
  - Construction (Design and Management) Regulations 2015
2. Approved Documents (England)
Core prescriptive compliance guidance:
  - Approved Document B (Volumes 1 & 2)
  - Approved Documents F, K, M, 7, and L
3. Fire Engineering Methodology
  - BS 7974 and the PD 7974 series (Parts 0 through 7)
4. Fire Safety Design of Buildings
  - BS 9999 (Design and management)
  - BS 9991 (Residential)
5. Fire Detection & Alarm Systems
  - BS 5839 series (Parts 1, 6, 8, and 9)
  - EN 54 series (Alarm Equipment)
6. Smoke Control & Ventilation
  - EN 12101 series (Parts 1–10)
  - BS 7346-4 and 7346-8
7. Fire Suppression Systems
  - BS EN 12845 and BS 9251 (Sprinklers)
  - BS 8458 (Water Mist)
  - BS EN 15004 series (Gas)
8. Emergency Lighting
