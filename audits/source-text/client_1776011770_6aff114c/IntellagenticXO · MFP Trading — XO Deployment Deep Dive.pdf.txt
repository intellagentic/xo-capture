⚠  ADDITION — MINERVA PLATFORM (PREVIOUSLY MISSING FROM ALL CHARTS)
MFP operates a proprietary platform called Minerva — not in any of the current charts or the working brief.
Minerva is MFP's core commercial differentiator: it acts as a passive bridge between Prime-of-Prime and retail
broker interest on one side and institutional liquidity on the other, converting hedging from a cost centre into a
revenue engine. The XO does not touch Minerva's trading logic — but our architecture diagrams need to show
it correctly positioned alongside TraderTools, otherwise the technical framing is incomplete.
⚠  CORRECTION — EXCEPTION SCOPE: 5 TYPES, NOT 6
The working brief lists 6 critical issues. The source material — Lisa's April 10 email, which is the official scope
document — lists exactly 5 exception types. The 6th item (key-person dependency) is the meta-problem that
motivates all 5, not a discrete exception. Framing it as a 6th "issue" dilutes the clarity of the deployment scope.
